About Abercrombie & Fitch

Abercrombie & Fitch is a clothing company that was founded in 1892 in New York City. The company specializes in casual luxury apparel and accessories for men, women, and kids. Abercrombie & Fitch is known for its stylish and trendy clothing, as well as its distinctive branding and marketing campaigns. The company's motto is "Casual Luxury," which reflects its commitment to providing high-quality, stylish clothing that is comfortable and easy to wear. The Abercrombie & Fitch brand is also known for its signature logo, which features a moose emblem and is prominently displayed on many of its products.

Our Values

MISSION: Our mission is to inspire and empower its customers to express their individuality through fashion. Abercrombie & Fitch is committed to creating an inclusive, sustainable, and innovative brand that is focused on delivering exceptional experiences to its customers.

VISION: Our vision is to be a global brand that is recognized for its quality, style, and innovation, and to be a leader in the fashion industry.

Clothing and Accessories

Online Shopping

Customers can browse and purchase Abercrombie & Fitch products online through the company's website. The website also features a variety of helpful tools and resources, such as size charts and fit guides.

In-Store Shopping

Operates brick-and-mortar stores across the United States and internationally, where customers can browse and purchase products in person.



Personal Styling

Offers personal styling services in select stores, where customers can receive one-on-one assistance from a stylist in selecting outfits and putting together looks.

Gift Cards

Offers gift cards that can be purchased online or in stores and can be redeemed for products at any Abercrombie & Fitch location.

Customer Service

Abercrombie & Fitch has a dedicated customer service team that can assist with questions, concerns, and order inquiries via phone, email, or live chat.
